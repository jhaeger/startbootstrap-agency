Grey Leaf Media

Custom web and logo design.

I designed logos for clients, set up blogs, personal and professional websites, and store-fronts with e-commerce shopping cart. I focused primarily and obsessively over minimalist, clean design for this site. Client sites were appropriately designed for the mission each was to accomplish.

Check it out: http://greyleafmedia.com

Built in 2011 using Wordpress with custom theme. All images and graphics created in Photoshop

EspressoTrainer

Logo and Web Design client

EspressoTrainer is a website built for a specialty coffee industry consultant and coffee retail trainer. The purpose was to attract the uninitiated who have the dream of opening shop one day, and inspiring them to do great things in their communities.

Check it out: http://et.greyleafmedia.com

Built in 2012 using Wordpress, a custom child theme, and all images and graphics created in Photoshop.

Veronica Galey

Website and logo for an award-winning artist.

Veronica Galey is a talented artist who needed a website to showcase her artwork. The quote on her home page says, "My paintings reflect comfort, and I strive to create a feeling of peace and relaxation when my artwork is being viewed." It is this that I had in mind when designing a calm, relaxing theme to really showcase her work.

Check it out: http://veronicagaley.com

Built in 2011 using Wordpress. Logo and graphic design done in Photoshop.

AJ Coffee Company

Logo, Website, and E-commerce for whole bean sales.

Custom logo, Website, and E-commerce platform using WP E-commerce for online sales of roast-to-order specialty coffee. Includes unique "Coffee Academy" with custom graphics. Logo designed in collaboration with independent artist depicting coffee fruit and leaf to reflect the coffee company's no-frills, "all about the coffee" approach.

Check it out: http://ajcoffeeco.com

Built in 2012.
